I design environments — digital, physical, and conversational — where people slow down and make sense of who they are. Background in architecture and consulting. Now building exploration and decision tools, narrative workshops, and AI-mediated reflection systems. Based at Dartmouth College's Center for Career Design. Teaching at Kent State University. Feel free to explore the work and reach out if you'd like to have a conversation.
